About Me

About Me

My name is Mariane Suñas, a senior high school student from Bacolod, Bato. One of my greatest passions in life is cooking. From a young age, I have found joy and creativity in the kitchen, experimenting with different ingredients and flavors to create delicious dishes.

Cooking is more than just a hobby for me; it is a way to express myself and share my love with others. I enjoy exploring various cuisines, trying new recipes, and putting my own twist on classic dishes. Whether it's baking decadent desserts, preparing hearty meals, or crafting intricate appetizers, the process of cooking allows me to combine my artistic flair with my love for flavors and textures.

There is something magical about bringing together different ingredients, following a recipe or improvising, and witnessing the transformation of raw ingredients into a beautiful and delectable creation. The kitchen is my sanctuary, where I can let my imagination run wild and create culinary masterpieces that bring people together. Cooking not only nourishes the body but also brings joy and warmth to the soul, and I am thrilled to continue exploring the culinary world and sharing my passion with others.
